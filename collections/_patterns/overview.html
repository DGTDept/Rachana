Overview

Here you find common patterns that you can use to start building your site. These are baseline patterns and can be tweaked to suit your product or service, these have basic accessibility built in like autocomplete attribute for form fields where it is needed. Do refer back to WCAG to see if you need to add in more to meet certain standards that you are trying to achieve.
