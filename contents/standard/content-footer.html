Description

All Singapore Government websites shall adopt the following footer design, containing the following policy statements at the footer of all web pages.

The default website footer shall includes:

  • Agency Name
  • Agency Details - Contact, Feedback, FAQ and social icons
  • Website Details - Privacy, Terms of User, Rate this Service and Sitemap
