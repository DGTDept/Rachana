What is Blueprint?

Blueprint is a Design System that contains a collection of reusable UI Components, guided by clear Digital Services Standards, that can be assembled together to bulid any number of applications.


Apart from having a big box of UI Lego pieces that can be assembled in many different ways, Blueprint provides structure and meaning in a set of guidelines. It is not a generic web page, but the embodiment of a system of concepts.


With a guide on why and how to use them, our UI Components and Templates become easier to use and clearer to discern.

Our Design System

The power of scale

We studied what most of our interfaces are comprised of and broke them down into fundamental building blocks and work up from there. From those individual UI elements, we can combine them into standard modules and templates to form a relatively complex, distinct section of an interface.

Provides Better Scalability

As teams grow, designers tend to solve problems individually that can lead to a fragmented visual language. Blueprint provides the most accessible source of truth for product teams. It also helps to keep team members aligned.

Lower Overall Costs

By using a standard design system will keep you on budget by keeping your design and code overhead low, while still allowing you to grow and evolve your services.

Improves User Experience

Standardized components that are used consistently create a more predictable and easy to understand application. It allow you to spend less time focusing on visual and more time developing a better user experience.

Helps to Prototype Faster

Working within an exisiting design system allows you to put pieces together a lot faster when creating prototypes. This makes iterations quick and painless.

Reduces Design Debts

Design debt is made up of non-reusable and inconsistent styles which is hard to maintain. Blueprint is maintain separately by professionals so as to help manage such debts.

Provides Built-in Accessibility

Accessibility can be designed and implemented at the component level. This can help to improve your product's overall accessibility and compliance with our Digital Services Standards.

Digital Services Standards

Understanding not only the what, but the why behind our designs

Digital Services Standards establishes a set of standards and guidelines for Singapore Government websites and digital services.


If you are currently developing application for an agency, please get a latest copy of Digital Services Standards from the Project Manager.
