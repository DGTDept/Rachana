    Guidelines

    Main Nav should:

    • Have logo link to homepage
    • Divide links into groups of navigation options
    • Apply navigation choices structured through layout, typography and icons
    • Have everything visible at once without scrolling
    • Have menu options revealed on hover, click or tap
