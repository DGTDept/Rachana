A simple and easy to use CMS platform that is powered by sgds theme.
Installing sgds Theme
  1. Login to your WordPress dashboard
  2. Click on to Appearance > Themes.
  3. Once you are in the theme page, click on the Add New button at the top.
  4. In the next screen, click on upload Theme button at the top.
  5. You will be prompted to choose the zip file. Select the sgds-wp-theme.zip and click Install Now.
  6. Once the sgds theme is installed, you will see a success message along with the link to activate and preview the theme.
