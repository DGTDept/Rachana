Guidelines

Masthead should:

  • Contain the Singapore Government Online Logo, which shall be positioned at the top left hand corner of the banner
  • Include the official government site descriptor, which shall state, 'A Singapore Government Agency Website'
  • Be positioned at the top-most left corner of the web page
