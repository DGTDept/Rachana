Agile and Waterfall

Agile processes typically come in a series of sprints. Within the definition of Agile there are more refined approaches and one of the most popular is Scrum.

Waterfall is the more traditional approach to product development where it's carried out sequentially and is subsequently more rigid and arguably less effective.
