Sentimeter allows you to listen to your users, anticipating their needs and surpassing their expectations

Purpose

The goal of Sentimeter is:

  • To be everywhere
    Empower your users to share their voice on their opinion of your website or e-service
  • To be simple
    Designed to be salient yet non-intrusive and easy to complete
  • To be useful
    Learn about what your user likes and dislikes

How it works

  • Gather user sentiment
    Three smiley choices presented to user to survey their current sentiment of using the service
  • Personalised choices
    Depending on the choice for smiley made, user will be prompted for more actionable feedback
  • Thank you page
    To signify a successful submission

Reporting

  • Daily report
    Sentimeter sends what is important for agencies to know, such as responses that might need action
  • Weekly report
    Sentimeter consolidates weekly responses and generate the performance for that week
  • Monthly report
    Agencies can compare performance in the last months
